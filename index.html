Welcome to the Paris 2024 Olympics!

Global celebration of sports, unity, and culture, with Paris as the stage for the 2024 Olympic Games.

Paris 2024

The Paris 2024 Olympics, officially known as the Games of the XXXIII Olympiad, are currently taking place in Paris, France, from July 26 to August 11, 2024. This marks the third time Paris has hosted the Summer Olympics, following the previous editions in 1900 and 1924, making it a centennial return to the city.

Inclusivity in Sport

The Paris 2024 Olympics are featuring an exciting lineup of 32 sports and a total of 329 events, showcasing a diverse array of athletic disciplines. From classic sports like athletics, swimming, and gymnastics to the debut of breaking (breakdance), the Games are blending tradition with innovation. Athletes from around the globe are competing for glory across iconic venues in and around Paris, highlighting both individual and team excellence.

Next generation

The Paris 2024 Olympics aim to inspire and engage younger generations worldwide. By introducing contemporary sports like breaking and enhancing digital platforms, the Games are designed to resonate with today’s youth. Paris 2024 also encourages young people to participate in sports through initiatives that emphasize accessibility, sustainability, and inclusion. These efforts strive to foster a lasting connection between the Olympic spirit and the next generation, empowering them to embrace the values of excellence, friendship, and respect.
